Who are we?

MiVi Beauty is a place where you are at the center. Our goal is for you to feel cared for, seen, and heard — and to leave your treatment feeling a little more satisfied than you expected.

We want the atmosphere in our salon to feel safe and relaxed, almost like a second home. With us, you are always welcome to ask questions, get help with skin or beauty-related concerns, share what’s on your mind — or simply enjoy a calm moment for yourself.

We also truly appreciate all feedback. Hearing what feels good and what we can continue to improve is invaluable to us.

A warm welcome to MiVi Beauty🤍

Julia

Beautician & Janssen Cosmetics ADVANCED Skincare Specialist

I am 21 years old, originally from Nagu, and I graduated as a SKY-certified cosmetologist in February 2024. After graduating, I worked for 9 months at Terapihörnet before opening my own salon, MiVi Beauty, in November 2024. Alongside running my own business, I also work as a cosmetologist at Pargas Apotek, where I have worked on and off since 2023.

During my first year as an entrepreneur, I have participated in several training courses and further education programs, including earning my Janssen Cosmetics ADVANCED Skincare Specialist certificate and attending a product training in Germany together with the Janssen Cosmetics team.

Some may recognize me from Terapihörnet, where I worked between January 2, 2024 and October 9, 2024.

Facial treatments as well as lash and brow services are my absolute strongest areas, but I love the variety of the profession and enjoy all types of treatments. As a person, I am cheerful, positive, and calm. I love to grow and learn new things and am always happy to help. If I don’t already know the answer, I will find out — and then we’ll learn together.

I provide service in Swedish, Finnish and English.

Adelene

Beautician

I graduated as a beautician on May 30, 2025, and I am passionate about both skincare and overall well-being. I am 20 years old and come from the Archipelago of Pargas. As a person, I am cheerful, energetic, and social, and I love meeting new people and helping them feel good.

During my education, I completed several internship periods, one of which was especially educational at Viking Line within the Wellness Spa department. There, I deepened my skills in areas such as facial massage, compress techniques, and full-body massage experiences that I carry with me into every treatment.

My favorite treatments are foot care services and various types of facial treatments.

I provide service in Swedish and English, and also Finnish to the extent possible
